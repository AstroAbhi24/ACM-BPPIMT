Inauguration of ACM Student Chapter at BPPIMT

We are thrilled to announce the grand inauguration of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) Student Chapter at B.P. Poddar Institute of Management and Technology (BPPIMT). This momentous occasion will mark the beginning of a new era in fostering technological innovation and academic excellence among our student community.

Date

July 22, 2023

Time

1 pm

Venue

Seminar Hall

About ACM Student Chapter at BPPIMT:

The ACM Student Chapter at BPPIMT is an initiative driven by a passionate group of students and faculty who share a common goal - to create a dynamic platform that nurtures the spirit of learning and collaboration in the field of computer science and technology. Our chapter aims to provide a thriving environment where students can enhance their technical skills, engage in exciting projects, and network with like-minded individuals.

Distinguished Chief Guest - Prof. Khalid Saeed:

We are honored to have Prof. Khalid Saeed, a renowned expert in the realm of computer science and engineering, as our esteemed chief guest for the inauguration ceremony. Prof. Saeed brings a wealth of knowledge and experience from his position at Bialystok University of Technology, Poland. His groundbreaking contributions to the field have earned him international acclaim, and we are delighted to have him grace our event.

Event Highlights:

Inspirational keynote address by Prof. Khalid Saeed Introduction to the ACM Student Chapter and its objectives Interactive sessions with faculty mentors Opportunities for students to join various technical committees Networking and socializing with fellow tech enthusiasts

Join us for a Remarkable Beginning:

We extend a warm invitation to all students, faculty members, and technology enthusiasts to join us on this momentous day. The inauguration of the ACM Student Chapter promises to be an enlightening and invigorating event that will set the stage for a journey filled with knowledge, growth, and innovation.

Let's come together and embrace the endless possibilities that lie ahead!
