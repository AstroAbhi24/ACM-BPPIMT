ABOUT ACM

ACM stands for the Association for Computing Machinery. It is an international professional organization dedicated to advancing computing as a science and profession. ACM was founded in 1947 and is one of the oldest and most prestigious organizations in the field of computer science.

What ACM offers?

Networking

ACM student chapters provide a platform for students to connect with their peers who share similar interests in computing.

Competitions and Hackathons

Many ACM student chapters participate in or organize coding competitions, hackathons, and programming contests

Access to Resources

Being affiliated with ACM grants students access to the ACM Digital Library, which is a vast repository of research papers, conference proceedings, and technical publications in the computing field
