More About US

Welcome to the official webpage of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) Student Chapter at B.P. Poddar Institute of Management & Technology (BPPIMT). We are a vibrant community of passionate students who are enthusiastic about all things related to computing and technology. Our chapter is dedicated to fostering academic, professional, and personal growth among students by providing them with numerous opportunities and resources in the field of computer science and technology.

Our Mission

Our mission is to cultivate a thriving community of aspiring technologists and innovators, inspiring them to excel in their academic pursuits and empowering them to become leaders in the ever-evolving world of computing. We aim to create a platform that encourages collaboration, knowledge-sharing, and skill development, enabling our members to make a significant impact in the global technological landscape.
