ACM BPPIMT STUDENT CHAPTER INAUGURATION

About the Seminar

We are excited to announce the successful inauguration of the ACM Student Chapter at BPPIMT, marked by an insightful seminar on "Biometrics and Its Applications in Various Industries" featuring Prof. Khalid Saeed as our esteemed guest speaker.

Prof. Dr. Khalid Saeed

Event Highlights

In this captivating seminar, Prof. Khalid Saeed, a distinguished expert in the field of biometrics, delved into the world of secure identity verification and authentication. The seminar provided an in-depth understanding of biometric technologies, exploring their underlying principles and advancements.

The ACM Student Chapter at BPPIMT

With the successful inauguration of the ACM Student Chapter, we are thrilled to embark on a journey of learning, collaboration, and innovation. The chapter aims to provide a platform for students and faculty to explore emerging trends in computer science and technology. We express our sincere gratitude to Prof. Khalid Saeed for sharing his expertise and inspiring us with his profound knowledge on biometrics. We extend our heartfelt thanks to all the participants who made this event a resounding success. Stay tuned for more exciting events and initiatives by the ACM Student Chapter at BPPIMT. We are committed to fostering a thriving community of technology enthusiasts, providing opportunities for growth, and making a positive impact on society.
